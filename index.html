Multi-Supplier Shopify Order Routing System

Production-Grade n8n Workflow for Print-on-Demand E-Commerce

The Challenge

A fast-growing POD store using three suppliers (Printful, Gooten, Printify) faced a 4-6 hour daily manual process of sorting, reformatting, and routing orders from a single Shopify export. This led to errors, delayed shipments, and customer complaints during peak sales.

The Solution

Built a fully autonomous n8n workflow with production-grade error handling, retry logic, real-time validation, centralized logging, and instant Slack alerts. The system processes exports automatically with zero human intervention under normal conditions.
